# 少昊导航 Promo Design

## Brand Summary
少昊导航是一个个人效率起始页，把常用网站、搜索入口、博客记录和工作流工具集中到一个固定坐标。

## Visual Direction
深色玻璃质感、低噪声背景、青绿色信号光、轻量网格和悬浮面板。画面应像一个安静但高效的工作台。

## Colors
- Canvas: `#071019`
- Heading: `#f4fbff`
- Body: `#d9e6ee`
- Muted: `#8ea2ae`
- Brand: `#55d0b7`
- Brand strong: `#8bf0da`
- CTA surface: `#dffaf4`

## Typography
- Primary: `IBM Plex Sans`
- Chinese fallback: `Noto Sans SC`, `Segoe UI`
- Display weight: 800
- Body weight: 400 to 500

## Components
- Glass panels: 24px radius, translucent dark fill, soft border, blurred backdrop.
- Signal pills: rounded labels with brand color and restrained letter spacing.
- Site cards: compact icon, title, short description, never decorative-only.
- Progress bar: thin, bottom anchored, brand gradient.

## Motion Rules
- Each scene enters with staggered typography and panel motion.
- Use overexposure wipe transitions between scenes.
- Keep background motion subtle: slow scale and grid opacity only.
- Avoid jump cuts, static screenshots, and busy decorative particles.

## What NOT to Do
- Do not use generic blue SaaS styling.
- Do not turn the promo into a landing page explainer.
- Do not add unrelated stock imagery.
- Do not replace the real Chinese product copy with English filler.
